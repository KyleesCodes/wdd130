Overview

Purpose

We are a company driven to bringing the best water rafting experience to you. With us, you can experience a fun time in the water with friends, family, or with your company! A variety of locations and trips are offered by us that no other company can give you. Join us for a rafting experience that will have you coming back for more!

Audience

Here you will find all the things you will need to know about rafting as well as our booking information! This activity is perfect for parties, business outings, vacations, and much more. Are you ready to get active in the water? Then let's go!

  • Where can I find information regarding safety?

    • You can find information regarding safety and other concerns in our FAQ!

  • What kind of items do I need to bring with me?

    • You will want to bring sunscreen, towels, your swimsuit, and bug repellent if you wish to keep bugs away from you. If you have any allergies or other health concerns, please bring your medicine and reach out to us directly prior to booking to ensure we can accomodate your needs.

  • How do I book my trip?

    • To book your trip, simply click our "book here" button below and follow the prompts! Be sure to throughly read through all of our information. If you get stuck during your booking, simply click the little help chat on the bottom right of your screen and we will guide you through the booking. Thank you for booking with us!
